How it works

What is a Parent-Provided Curriculum?
Arizona ESA parents must submit a curriculum showing why their purchases meet AZ DOE criteria. Required for all purchases beyond auto-approved items.
Do I still need to submit receipts?
Yes. This generates the curriculum justification. Receipts still go through ClassWallet. The curriculum supports your receipts with an educational rationale.
Is this approved by AZ DOE?
This tool formats your curriculum following the official template. You are responsible for accuracy.
