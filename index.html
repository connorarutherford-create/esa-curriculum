About PPCs

What is a Parent-Provided Curriculum?
A document Arizona ESA parents submit showing why their purchases meet educational criteria. Required for purchases beyond auto-approved items.
What happens if it's rejected?
There is no formal "rejection" process — the PPC is submitted with your receipt documentation. A clear, detailed PPC protects you in the case of an audit.
Is there an official template?
The AZ DOE provides a simple fillable form with 6 fields, but the community has developed more thorough formats like this one. The more detail you provide, the stronger your documentation.
