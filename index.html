How it works

What is a Parent-Provided Curriculum?
Arizona ESA parents must submit a curriculum showing why their purchases meet the AZ Department of Education criteria. This form is required for all ESA purchases beyond auto-approved items.
What makes a purchase "allowable"?
It must be directly related to a course of study. A desk supports focused study time. Art supplies support a visual arts curriculum. A microscope supports science observation. Our generator connects your purchase to a valid learning objective.
Is this approved by the AZ Department of Education?
This tool helps you format your curriculum following the official template. You are responsible for ensuring the content accurately reflects your child's education.
What about the ESA debit card receipts?
The curriculum covers the "why." You still need to submit receipts through ClassWallet. This tool helps you create the curriculum justification that supports those receipts.
