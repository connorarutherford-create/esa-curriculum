## Additional Details

**Is this approved by the Arizona Department of Education?**
The ESA program accepts Parent-Provided Curriculums submitted by families. This tool helps you write one that meets the community-standard format. You submit it through your regular ESA account — we don't file on your behalf.

**What if my curriculum is rejected?**
Every PPC is a living document. If the DOE asks for revisions, you can edit and recompile in seconds. The tool doesn't lock you into a final version.

**Do I need a Gemini API key?**
The tool uses Gemini for AI generation. Pro users are prompted to enter their own free API key from Google AI Studio (gemini.google.com). The free tier includes 60 requests per minute — more than enough for personal use.

**Is my data private?**
Everything runs in your browser. No data is sent to our servers — only to Google's Gemini API (if you use AI generation). Your curriculums, student names, and purchase details never touch our infrastructure.

**Can I use this for multiple students?**
Yes. Unlimited PPCs for all your students under one license.

**What if I need help?**
The app has a built-in help panel with step-specific tips. Click "Need help?" at the top of any step. If you're stuck, email connorarutherford@gmail.com.
